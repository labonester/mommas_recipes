Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (1/2 a package)
  • Tomato sauce (2 cans)
  • El pato (1/2 can or less depending on how hot you want)
  • 1 small onion or 1/2 of a medium onion
  • 1 packet of goya powder (tomato and cilantro or cilantro flavor)
  • 2 tbsp sour cream (as much as you want, but start with 2 tbsp)

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti
  2. While noodles boil, slice onion into thick rings and saute in butter, stop before butter burns
  3. Once onions cooked, add to same pan: the tomato sauce, el pato sauce and goya packet
  4. When noodles are done (you decide how soft you want), rinse and drain
  5. Add sauce to noodles and stir, then add sour cream to taste
