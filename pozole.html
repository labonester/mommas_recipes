Ingredients

  • 3 or 4 pounds depending on the size of the pot of pork neck bones
  • 1 pound of boneless pork chops, not the leanest white meat ones
  • 2 29oz cans of cooked hominy
  • 2 small cans of pinto beans
  • Menudo mix
  • Red chili powder
  • Garlic (~5 cloves)
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Put bones, pork chops and garlic cloves in pot with water, cover and boil for about 1 1/2 hour, check after 1 hour, add more water if low
  2. After 1 1/2 hour, add the hominy, chili powder, salt and packet of menudo mix and boil for about another hour, then add beans, bring to boil again, then it should be ready
