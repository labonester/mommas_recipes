Ingredients

  • 1 bag of lentils
  • 1 lb. of hamburger meat
  • 1 small tomato
  • 1/2 onion (medium)
  • 2 serrano peppers
  • 1 8oz can of tomato sauce
  • Chicken broth powder (2 tbsp)
  • Salt
  • 1 packet of goya powder

Instructions

  1. Put water and lentils to boil (about 45 minutes), add salt to water, about 1 tbsp
  2. While that's going, start chopping onion, tomato, and serrano pepper
  3. Put veggies in frying pan along with raw hamburger meat and cook until meat is brown
  4. Once lentils have been boiling for at least 1/2 hour, check to see if they are still hard; if they are, keep boiling
  5. When lentils are soft enough to math between your fingers, add meat, tomato sauce, chicken broth, and goya powder. Also add water a little at a time until you like the consistency
  6. Stir and taste so that you can start adding salt, keep adding salt until you like the taste and then let boil on medium high heat for about 20-25 minutes
